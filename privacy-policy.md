# Privacy Policy – Aatu

Last updated: 12.15.2025

1. Purpose of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KeloTech (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in connection with the Aatu mobile application (“the App”).

We respect user privacy and aim to be transparent about our practices.

2. Data Collection

Aatu does not require user accounts and does not collect or store personal data such as names, email addresses, or profile information.

The App can be used without registration.

3. Payments

The App may offer paid features or in-app purchases.

All payments are processed exclusively by the platform providers (such as Apple App Store or Google Play Store).
We do not collect, process, or store any payment information, including credit card details or billing data.

Payment data is handled directly by the platform according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

All game-related data (such as game state or settings) is stored locally on the user’s device.

We do not operate our own backend servers for user data storage.

5. Third-Party Services

Aside from mandatory platform services required for app distribution and payment processing, Aatu does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

6. Children

The App is intended for users 18 years of age or older.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be effective immediately upon publication within the App or its store listing.
